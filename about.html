About Me!

I am currently a Junior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison and I'm pursuing a degree in Consumer Behavior & Marketplace Studies along with a certificate in Digital Studies. In my free time I like to make Irish dance videos!

I hope to turn my hobby of creating videos into a career one day. I am currently taking classes that revolve around technology and creativity which I have enjoyed thus far.
